The 'Evolutionary Nomad' Ladder to Success

   2014-04-02      complex systems · evolutionary algorithms

The misty peaks of Krabi, Thailand. (Credit: Vyacheslav Argenberg) The road to success is dead. Long live the roads to success. Back in 2012, The Wall Street Journal announced that Goldman Sachs would be scaling back its junior analyst program. Two year contracts for college grads? Gone. A bonus for completing the program? Nope. For a generation, these analyst positions were a secure first step onto the ladder of success for many financial graduates.
